Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

fontloom.com ("we", "the site") provides a free browser-based tool for converting text into stylized Unicode characters. This page explains what data is and isn't collected when you use the site.

How the tool works

The fancy text generator runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Text you type is transformed locally on your device and is never transmitted to, or stored on, any server operated by this site. Closing the tab discards everything you typed.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertisements served by third-party ad networks, such as Google AdSense. These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or generally at aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic (e.g. page views, approximate location by country, device type). This data is not linked to the text you generate on the site.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the site owner via the contact link in the footer.
